Works best in larger groups 4+ coworkers

  • Analyze coworkers behavior for months on end
  • Write down 25 behaviors, phrases, or interactions
  • Place in 5x5 grid
  • Observe and check off as bingo items are completed
  • Laughing encouraged
  • First player to complete 5 items in a row, column, or diagonally stands up from their desk and yells "I've discovered a priority 0 issue".
